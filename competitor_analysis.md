# Competitor & Pricing Analysis: GCC WhatsApp Automation

### 1. Full-Service & Bespoke Automation Agencies

**Prism Digital**
*   **Scope of Work:** Bespoke WhatsApp API solutions. Custom, multilingual AI-driven chatbots and CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot) for automated lead qualification.
*   **Pricing:**
    *   **Setup:** AED 1,500 - AED 5,000 (starts), complex enterprise builds exceeding AED 50,000.
    *   **Maintenance:** Custom monthly retainers.

**NaulX & RW Infotech (n8n/Make Specialists)**
*   **Scope of Work:** Specialized automation focusing heavily on Make and n8n (popular for UAE data residency compliance). They conduct process audits and build custom API nodes.
*   **Pricing:**
    *   **Setup:** AED 5,000 to AED 50,000+ depending on system complexity.
    *   **Maintenance:** Monthly service contracts for managing self-hosted n8n instances.

**Octopus Marketing**
*   **Scope of Work:** Targeted WhatsApp marketing and automation, API provisioning, and lead-gen chatbot design.
*   **Pricing:** Custom quotes based on complexity.

### 2. Localized SaaS Platforms (DIY / Hybrid)

**Rasayel**
*   **Scope:** WhatsApp automation SaaS with a strong presence in the GCC. Shared team inbox, visual chatbot builder.
*   **Pricing (Platform Only):** $150/month (Start Plan), $400/month (Grow Plan), Enterprise starts at $2,000/month.

**Wati**
*   **Scope:** Global WhatsApp BSP with no-code automation flow builders and AI agents for lead qualification.
*   **Pricing (Platform Only):** $69/month (Growth), $149/month (Pro - includes AI), $349/month (Business).

### 3. Meta WhatsApp API Usage Fees (Applies to all)
Regardless of whether a client uses a custom agency or a SaaS tool, they must pay Meta directly for message usage:
*   **Marketing Messages:** Highest tier (~AED 0.10 - 0.40+ per message depending on the destination country).
*   **Utility & Authentication Messages:** Lower cost.
*   **Service Messages:** Free within an open 24-hour customer service window initiated by the customer.
